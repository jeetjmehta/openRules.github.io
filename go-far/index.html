Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation facts
What is Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation for? The artificial substitution of heart and lung action for cardiac arrest. The two major components of cardiopulmonary resuscitation are artificial respiration and closed-chest cardiac massage.
Who should consider Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)? Individuals who want to live if their heart or lung stops.
Who should NOT undergo CPR? Individuals who do NOT want to live if their heart or lung stops.
